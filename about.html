About SecureTix

Revolutionizing event ticketing with cryptographic security

Our Mission

SecureTix is pioneering the future of event ticketing by implementing Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) technology to eliminate fraud, ensure authenticity, and provide unparalleled security for event-goers worldwide.

Traditional ticketing systems are vulnerable to counterfeiting, scalping, and fraud. SecureTix addresses these challenges with battle-tested cryptographic principles used by banks, governments, and Fortune 500 companies.

Core Security Principles

Confidentiality

End-to-end encryption ensures your ticket data remains private

Integrity

Cryptographic hashing detects any tampering attempts

Authentication

Digital certificates prove the identity of ticket holders

Non-Repudiation

Digital signatures provide proof of ticket ownership

Technology Stack

  • RSA-2048: Industry-standard asymmetric encryption
  • AES-256: Advanced symmetric encryption for data
  • SHA-256: Cryptographic hash function for integrity
  • X.509: Standard digital certificate format
  • PBKDF2: Key derivation for PIN protection
  • Forge.js: Comprehensive PKI implementation

Real-World Applications

PKI technology similar to SecureTix is used in:

Banking & Finance

Secure online transactions, digital signatures for contracts, SSL/TLS certificates

Healthcare

Patient record protection, HIPAA compliance, secure medical data exchange

Government

Citizen identification, secure communications, document signing
